                General Questions

                Is Charity A Recognized Organization ?

                Charity is a recognized fund raising non-government organization that is specially working for the welfare of African children.

                How Can I View Your Annual Report To Know That How You Use Our Money?

                Everyone can access the annual report from our website or can have a printed copy of financial report from the nearest office.

                Don’t Government Provide Any Support?

                Charity is a non-government organization. We do not get any fund directly from the government, except exemption on various taxes. This minimizes our expenses and other costs.

                How Does Charity Covers Its Own Expenses?

                Our expenses are very little that include, traveling, managing resources, collecting donation, monitoring activity and advertising. For our office management such as information system, furniture and other requirement, we get support from our partner at free or discounted rate.

                Why Does Charity Not Provide Information About Children?

                We use donors’ fund in all the resources and our programs to carry out the children’s holistic development and other welfares. Hence, there is no use of sharing name of children. To know about how do we use your fund, you can become our partner and explore that how our team work.

                Does Charity Provide Information About Where My Donation Is Used?

                There are thousands of people, who make contribution in our projects. Therefore, it is not easy for us to prepare report regarding particular individual that comprises where his money is used. But, you can see the financial report on our website.

                How Does Your Team Raise Fund For Resources?

                We have partnered with major organization & institutions as well as individual that provide us all the resources at heavy discount.

                What Method Do Charity Use To Choose Project For Its Support?

                Our team comprises the experts of different backgrounds, including economist, marketing strategist, health care business, Journalist, financial management and others. They first scrutinize every aspect of the project and after finding it suitable, they start working on it.

                General Questions

                Does Charity Employees Get Salary?

                Every full-time employee gets salary here. Our team believes that this is the best way to bring the talented professional here. Here, all the employees are paid modest salary as compared to the other organization.

                Will My Little Contribution Be Helpful For You?

                Although the problem is huge, everyone’s little contribution will add up to become huge amount that can be invested to carry out the project and campaign in support of children welfare smoothly.

                How Does Charity Work To Ensure Child Rights?

                We work with different communities and other NGOs to make children aware of their basic rights and help them access education and imperative healthy care needs.

                What Are Modes Of Making Donation?

                In order to make it convenience for all the people, who want to donate their funds, there are different online payment modes, like net banking, credit and debit card system. Moreover, the user can also make donation through Pay Pal account.

                How Can I Make Donation?

                You can transfer your fund to us either by becoming partner with us or just go to our home page; there you will see different “Donate Now” options for our different programs. Just choose anyone and makes donation.

                Is Your Transaction Architecture Secure?

                Our online transaction system is completely secure; that no one can breach easily to hack all information regarding net banking, credit card and debit card.

                How Do You Help Improve Medical Care?

                We are working with various NGOs and healthcare organizations that help us improve medical facility. We also get imperative medicines to curb the shortage of medicines.

                How Do Sponsorship Help My Business?

                We are growing organization. Our team organizes various special and enjoyable events that grab attention of millions and this will automatically make your brand visible.
